Eggs 5 medium Milk 750 ml Nuts 50g Strawberry Sauce 20ml

Remove yolk from 3 eggs and beat until fluffy.

Add milk gradually and mix well.

Freeze for one hour.

When serving sprinkle with nuts and cover in strawberry sauce.
